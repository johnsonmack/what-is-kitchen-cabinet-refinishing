Why Choose Cabinet Refinishing?

When your kitchen starts to feel outdated, replacing cabinets isn't your only option. Kitchen cabinet refinishing refreshes your space at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

What is Kitchen Cabinet Refinishing?

Refinishing involves sanding, repairing, priming, and repainting or staining your existing cabinets. This restores beauty and functionality without the expense of new cabinetry.

Techniques:

  • Painting: Apply fresh paint for a modern look.
  • Staining: Highlight natural wood grains.
  • Glazing: Add depth and texture.
  • Sealing: Protect with durable finishes.

Key Benefits

  • Save 50-70% compared to replacements
  • Quick turnaround with minimal disruption
  • Eco-friendly with less waste
  • Fully customizable colors and finishes
  • Boosts home value and appeal

Refinishing Process

  1. Assessment and cleaning
  2. Sanding and repairs
  3. Priming
  4. Painting or staining
  5. Sealing for durability
  6. Reassembly and quality check

DIY vs Professional Refinishing

DIY:

  • Cheaper if you have tools
  • Time-consuming and labor-intensive
  • Risk of uneven results

Professional Services:

  • Expert quality finish
